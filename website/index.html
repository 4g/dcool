How does it work ?

We collect data from all sensors of your data center by integrating with BMS systems. With data based digital twins that can run millions of simulations per hour, we find optimal cooling policies for every situation. By combining these twins with state of the art Reinforcement Learning algorithms, we derive optimal strategies that can keep your DC cool while keeping it safe.
